About Me

Hello! I'm Kenechukwu — an Electronic Engineering graduate from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, currently pursuing a second degree in Computer Science at the University of the People. My path into tech didn't start with code; it started with substations, SCADA screens, and telecom sites.

I've spent the last two years working across Nigeria's energy infrastructure — from transmission substations with TCN to hybrid power operations at IHS Towers — where I learned to turn messy operational data into dashboards people actually use. That experience is what drew me to web development: I want to build the clean, human-facing side of the tools I've only ever worked behind.

Right now I'm mastering the fundamentals — semantic HTML, modern CSS layouts, and Git — on my way to becoming a full-stack developer. When I'm not coding, I'm reading, learning new words, and sharpening how I communicate ideas clearly.
